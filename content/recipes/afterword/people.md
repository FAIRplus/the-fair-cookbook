# Boards and contributors

+++
---

 <p style='text-align: justify;'>
The FAIR Cookbook is coordinated by an Editorial Board, assisted by Section Editors; a growing number of Contributors write and review recipes, or contribute to the work the recipes describe. 

If you are a researcher or a data managers professional, with expertise and experience in FAIR data management, and you want to [contribute, see how to,](fcb-misc-contribute) or contact us at [faircookbook-ed@elixir-europe.org](mailto:faircookbook-ed@elixir-europe.org)
 </p>
 
---
